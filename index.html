01

Introduction

Overview of our commitment

Rentyfy ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application "Rentyfy" (the "App"), available on Google Play Store.

ℹ️ Important

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

02

Information We Collect

What data we gather and how

A. Personal Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Address / Locality (for property listing or search)
  • Property details you post (photos, description, price, location etc.)

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, OS version, unique device ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
  • Location data (only when you allow and use location-based search or posting)

C. Third-Party Services

🔗 Firebase by Google

We use Google Firebase for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, and cloud storage. Firebase may collect limited device and usage data as per their privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

03

How We Use Your Information

Purpose of data collection

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow you to post, search, and view properties
  • Enable chat/communication between users
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important notifications (inquiries, messages, updates)
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
04

Sharing of Information

When and with whom we share data
🛡️ Our Promise

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these cases:

  • With other users (your name, phone number, chat messages) when you post a property or send inquiry
  • With service providers (Firebase, Google Cloud) for hosting and analytics
  • If required by law, court order, or government request
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property
05

Location Data

How we handle location information

We collect precise location only when you enable it for searching nearby properties or posting location-specific listings. You can disable location permission anytime from your device settings.

📍 Location Control

You have full control. Go to your device Settings → Apps → Rentyfy → Permissions to manage location access at any time.

06

Data Retention

How long we keep your data

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

Data Type Retention Period
Account Data Until you delete your account
Posted Properties Until you delete them or account is deleted
Chat Messages 90 days after last activity (or as per user deletion)

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure cloud infrastructure powered by Google Firebase.

09

Children's Privacy

Protection for minors

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

🔒 Child Safety

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.
